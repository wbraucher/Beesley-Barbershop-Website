About the Shop

One chair. One barber. The same way it has always been done.

The shop

We have been doing this a long time.

Beesley's has been in Midvale for decades. The shop has always been one chair, one barber, and a straightforward approach to the work. We do not have a lot of product displays or an app or a loyalty rewards program. We have scissors, clippers, a straight razor, and some hot towels. That covers most of what people actually need.

Midvale is the kind of neighborhood where people still go to the same barber their whole lives. We see regulars who started coming in as kids and now bring their own kids. We know what they like, how their hair grows, and which way it does not cooperate. That kind of history takes years to build and we think it is worth keeping.

We are not trying to compete with the big franchise shops or the trendy places downtown. They are fine for what they are. This shop is for people who want a clean haircut from someone who actually knows them, at a fair price, without any of the extra stuff. If that sounds like you, come in and sit down.

Why people come back

What makes us different

No gimmicks.

We do not have a subscription service or a phone app or a color-coded menu of add-ons. You come in, you tell us what you want, we cut your hair. That is the whole thing. It has been working for a long time and we are not planning to change it.

Walk-ins always welcome.

We take appointments online and we also take walk-ins, every single day we are open. No shame in just showing up. If there is a wait, we will tell you how long it is and you can decide from there. We have never turned anyone away just because they did not book ahead.

Real straight razor shaves.

Not a cartridge razor with shaving cream from a can. A proper straight razor shave with hot towel prep and a cool towel finish. It takes 45 minutes and it is one of the better things you can do for yourself on a Saturday morning. People who try it once tend to make it a regular thing.

Come see us.

Book online or just walk in. We are open Tuesday through Saturday, 7355 S 900 E in Midvale.
