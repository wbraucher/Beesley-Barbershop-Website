Our Flagship Services

These are the services we get asked about most. Here is what to expect when you sit down.

Service 01

Haircut

We start by talking through what you want. Scissor or clipper cut, whatever fits your style and how your hair actually grows. No cookie-cutter approach and no rushing you out the door. When you are done, you will know exactly what to ask for next time.

Price $27
Duration 30 min
Book a Haircut

Service 02

Hot Towel Straight Razor Shave

This is the real thing. We start with a hot towel to open up your pores and soften the hair, then work through the shave with a straight razor. A cool towel at the end closes everything back up and leaves your skin feeling like it should. Takes about 45 minutes. People who have tried it tend to come back for it specifically.

Price $40
Duration 45 min
Book a Shave

Service 03

Beard Trim

Bring in a reference photo or just describe what you are going for and we will shape it up from there. Price depends on how long it is and how much work it needs. We will tell you the exact amount before we start. Most trims land between $10 and $20 and take about 20 minutes.

Price $10–$20
Duration 20 min
