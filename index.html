Who we are

We've been cutting hair in this neighborhood for a long time. No fancy stuff, no upsells. Just a good cut and a conversation if you want one.

Beesley's is a one-chair shop in Midvale where the same faces show up week after week. We know our regulars by name and their kids by sight. If it's your first time, you'll be treated the same as someone who's been coming for twenty years. That's the whole idea.
